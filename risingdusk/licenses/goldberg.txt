Goldberg Steam Emulator
https://gitlab.com/Mr_Goldberg/goldberg_steam_emu

The files steam_api.dll and steam_api64.dll bundled in this port are from the
Goldberg Steam Emulator. They are NOT Valve's Steam client libraries — they are
a clean-room reimplementation of the Steam API. They are included so the game
can initialise on a device where Steam is not installed. The original game files
(Rising Dusk.exe, assets/, etc.) are not included and must be obtained by
purchasing the game on Steam.
